Solar System quick facts

  • Sizes & distances are not to scale (compressed for visibility).
  • Earth orbits the Sun in ~365 days; Neptune ~165 years.
  • Saturn’s ring stylized; orbits shown as guides.
Built with Three.js · Educational scale model

Shortcuts & Tips

  • Rotate: drag left; Pan: right‑drag or Ctrl/Cmd + drag; Zoom: wheel/pinch.
  • Space: Play/Pause (Solar) or Toggle Auto‑rotate (Galaxy).
  • Z: Reset Zoom (keep direction) · R: Reset View (recenter).
  • U: Hide/Show UI for a clean view · ?/H: Toggle this help.
  • Solar: use Focus dropdown or click a planet. Lock camera to follow motion.
  • Galaxy: click the Core glow or Sol marker to focus. Use spin/tilt/brightness for clarity.
